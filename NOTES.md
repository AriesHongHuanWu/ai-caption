# Local Studio v0.1.39

🎹 **Compose tools** — the first step of turning Local Studio into a complete end-to-end production suite.

When you analyze a track (in the Downloader), it now doesn't just *tell* you the key — it hands you what to **play and write over it**:
- The **scale** + all **diatonic chords** (triads and 7ths) for the detected key.
- **Common chord progressions** spelled out in your key (Pop, Emo/Trap, ii–V–I, Andalusian…).
- **Borrowed chords** and the **relative** major/minor.
- A one-click **metronome click track** at the detected BPM, to write or record in time.

So: download a beat → analyze → see its key/BPM/structure/EQ **and** the chords + click to build your topline on.

This kicks off a roadmap to cover the whole musician workflow (compose → record → mix → master → deliver → visuals) as one studio — more coming.

Everything from v0.1.38 is included (own Downloader category + decluttered layout, file-first Toolbox, stronger mastering + 21 presets).
