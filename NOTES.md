# Local Studio v0.1.48

## ✂️ Pro timeline tools

- **Razor / blade tool** — switch to the blade and click any clip to split it exactly where you click.
- **Markers** — drop a marker at the playhead (clips and the playhead snap to them); click a marker to jump there, double-click to remove.
- **Snapshot** — save the current frame as a **PNG** with one click.
- **PiP frame** — give any clip a **drop shadow, border, and rounded corners** (great for picture-in-picture).

Part of the ongoing CapCut-parity expansion. Everything from v0.1.47 (on-canvas move/scale/rotate, camera shake, Ken Burns, GPU export) is included.
