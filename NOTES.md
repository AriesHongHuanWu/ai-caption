# Local Studio v0.2.2 — Mastering: pre-master FX

## 🎚️ New: pre-master processing (multi-select)
Under Mastering → Advanced you can now shape the song **before** the mastering chain runs — combine any of:

- **Trim the song** — master only a chosen section (start / end seconds).
- **Fade in / Fade out** — smooth the head and tail.
- **High-pass** — cut sub-bass rumble.
- **Low-pass** — soften the highs.

All applied locally, then your full AI mastering chain runs on the cleaned-up audio.

## Already there (in case you missed it)
Mastering also already gives you a **professional mix analysis** the moment you load a file — overall score, problem list, the **before/target/after spectrum**, the **AI auto-EQ curve**, loudness/dynamics, and the full **signal chain** in the result. Open the analysis panel to see the report and charts.
