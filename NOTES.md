# Local Studio v0.2.0 — major Editor update 🎬

A big step toward a professional NLE.

## ✨ Adjustment layer
Add an **Adjustment layer** (濾鏡 tab → ＋ 調整層) — a clip that **grades every clip beneath it**. Drop any look or filter onto it and it colours the whole stack below for its duration, just like Premiere's Adjustment Layer / CapCut's effect track. Move and trim it to control exactly when the grade applies.

## ✨ Transitions on the cut
Drag a transition from the library **onto the seam between two adjacent clips** (the ⋈ handle at the cut) and it's applied to both sides — the way a pro editor places transitions. (You can still drop a transition onto a single clip too.)

## ✨ Bigger creative libraries
- **18 looks** (＋ Teal-Orange, Pastel, Moody, Sunset, Matrix, Dreamy, Crisp, Cyberpunk…)
- **More one-tap effects** (VHS glitch, soft blur, circle / rounded masks…)
- **More title styles** (Pop, Shadow bar, Minimal…)

## Plus everything from the recent fixes
Clips move & trim smoothly · drag media onto the timeline with **auto-created tracks** · **drag-to-apply** effects · **applied-effects stack** in the inspector · forced-aligned **karaoke captions** · GPU export · WAV / PNG export.
